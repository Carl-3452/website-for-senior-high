Probability Equations Quiz

1. What is the probability of getting a head when flipping a fair coin?

2. A bag contains 3 red and 2 blue balls. What is the probability of picking a blue ball?

3. A die is rolled once. What is the probability of getting a number greater than 4?

4. What is the probability of drawing a king from a standard deck of 52 cards?

5. If you toss two coins, what is the probability of getting exactly one head?

6. A spinner has 8 equal sections numbered 1-8. What is the probability of landing on an even number?

7. A box has 10 balls numbered 1-10. What is the probability of picking a number divisible by 3?

8. A card is drawn from a deck. What is the probability of getting a red card (hearts or diamonds)?

9. A bag contains 4 red, 5 blue, and 1 green ball. What is the probability of not picking a red ball?

10. A die is rolled twice. What is the probability of getting a double (same number both times)?
